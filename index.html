Meal Planning Tips

Plan your meals on Sunday for the whole week. Write down what you want to eat each day.

Make a shopping list based on your meal plan. Include all ingredients you need for the week.

Prep vegetables on weekends. Cook rice and meat in big batches to use during the week.

About AfriMeal Planner

AfriMeal Planner is your companion for discovering, planning, and enjoying authentic African cuisine. Our mission is to make meal planning easy, healthy, and culturally rich by bringing the flavors of Africa to your kitchen.

Our Vision

We believe in celebrating Africa's diverse food heritage. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned cook, our platform helps you explore recipes, organize weekly meals, and learn about nutrition the African way.

Why Choose Us?

  • Curated recipes from across Africa
  • Easy-to-use meal planner
  • Nutrition tips and healthy eating guides
  • Interactive features for a fun experience

Meet the Team
